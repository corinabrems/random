Welcome to Beaver Stadium!

Beaver Stadium is the home of the Nittany Lions since 1960! Beaver Stadium is the 4th largest stadium in the world and the second largest stadium in the western hemisphere. Additionally, in 2016, Beaver Stadium was voted the number one stadium in all of college football.

The stadium is famous for its whiteout events, student section, and tailgates as well. Along with the blue band, it's no surprise that game days at Beaver Stadium are always packed with up to 100,000 fans!
