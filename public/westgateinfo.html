Welcome to the Westgate Building!

On Penn State's website for the College of IST, you'll see Visitor Information which provides you with maps and parking information. You can also find some basic information about the building.."the Westgate Building at Penn State University Park is a striking 199,000 square-foot structure whose sweeping lines stir the imagination of passersby. Its creators–Rafael Vinoly Architects, New York, and Perfido Weiskopf Architects, Pittsburgh—were inspired by the Ponte Vecchio in Florence."

Classrooms 'utilize a state-of-the-art' telecommucnications and multimedia infrastructure! This building includes a Cybertorium (which seats 155 students), a three-dimensional vizualization labratory, and a number of gathering spaces for students to study or for staff to meet!
