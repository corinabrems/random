Welcome to West Dorms!

West Campus has dorms right by the library and Westgate! This residential brick building houses 1680 students, 600 of those being first year students. West Dorms are more known for being there for upperclassmen, making it the smallest first-year living environment on campus!

This housing area offers a computer lab, a classroom, a ResCom office, a dining hall, a culutural lounge, and a commons desk!
